Who We Are

At Stairs and Tread Co, we specialise in high-quality external staircases and treads that combine durability, safety, and visual appeal. Based in Melbourne, we construct custom external staircases using structural treated pine. Built tough to withstand the elements and suited to all outdoor applications.

With over 10 years of experience in the building industry, we bring a wealth of knowledge to every project. This ensures that each staircase we build is not only engineered correctly but also meets the highest standards of strength and safety.

We use solid, long-lasting materials to deliver staircases and treads that are built to perform. Whether it's for a deck, garden, or commercial space, you can rely on us for outdoor stairs that are functional, reliable, and made to last.
